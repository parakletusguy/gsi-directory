Identity chip for researchers and users. Falls back to initials on a deterministic green tint.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Amina Okeke" />
<Avatar name="Luis Mendoza" src="/photo.jpg" size="lg" />
<Avatar name="DOAJ" square size="sm" />
```

- **size**: `xs` 24 · `sm` 32 · `md` 40 · `lg` 56
- `square` for non-person entities (sources, journals).
